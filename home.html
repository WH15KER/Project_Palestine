Fitzfrancis lledo

The Classic Beef Burger at Gia's Kitchnette is a true delight. Juicy, flavorful, and perfectly cooked, this burger is everything you want in a classic burger. The bun is soft yet sturdy, and the toppings are fresh and plentiful. The combination of lettuce, tomato, onion, and melted cheese complements the beef patty beautifully. It's a must-try for burger enthusiasts.

Nelvin San Pedro

If you're looking for a burger with a gourmet twist, look no further than Gia's Kitchnette. Their Gourmet Burger is a culinary masterpiece. Made with premium Angus Beef, caramelized onions, and arugula, each bite is a symphony of flavors. The beef is cooked to perfection, juicy and tender, and the truffle cheese adds a luxurious touch. It's a bit pricey, but worth every penny for the quality and taste.

Mark Daniel

Gia's Kitchnette the best Margherita Pizza in town. The crust is thin and crispy, the tomato sauce is flavorful without being overwhelming, and the fresh basil leaves add a fragrant herbal note. The mozzarella cheese is perfectly melted and gooey, tying everything together beautifully. It's a simple yet classic pizza done right.

Jerry Langit

If you're a carnivore at heart, the meat lover here at Gia's Kitchnette is sure to satisfy your cravings. Loaded with pepperoni, sausage, bacon, and ham, every bite is packed with savory goodness. The crust is thick and chewy, providing the perfect base for the generous toppings. It's a hearty and indulgent pizza that's perfect for sharing with friends or devouring all by yourself.

Javerielle Flores

Even meat lovers won't be able to resist the Vegetarian Supreme Pizza at Gia's Kitchnette. This pizza is loaded with a colorful array of fresh veggies, including bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, and tomatoes. The combination of flavors and textures is simply unbeatable. The crust is crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, providing the perfect canvas for the delicious toppings. It's a healthy and satisfying option for pizza lovers of all kinds.
